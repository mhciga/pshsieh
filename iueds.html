Instructional Designs for IU eDS

    • Assistant training material

    Accessibility analysis
    PDF/Word accessibility fix

  • In-charge ID

    • Kimberly Seeber

  • Project Link

    • *Please log into your IU account to view the files.
    J401-List of Inaccessible Files
    J404-List of Inaccessible Files

  • Summary

    • Based on the accessibility knowledge and skills I acquired from the eDS trainings and projects, I was able to audit and resolve the accessibility issues of the online courses J401 Global Classroom and J404 Strategic Management (Designers). I utilized Ally and PDF Accessibility tools to identify the inaccessible files, and then analyzed the factors that caused the inaccessibility. After this process, I compiled all the inaccessible files' information into a table with their file names, links, locations, Ally's accessibility scores and solutions. I sourced the necessary solutions from relevant resources and documents. Most of the files could be improved to a high or perfect accessibility level. For the remaining files, I have provided reasons why they could not be fixed for the instructor's reference. These reports demonstrate my ability to effectively address many common PDF accessibility issues.
