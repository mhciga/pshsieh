2021 IU OVPIT & UITS Organizational Chart

The image is an organizational chart of the Office of the Vice President for Information Technology (OVPIT) and the University Information Technology Services (UITS) at Indiana University (IU) in 2021. The organization generated $4 billion in revenue and a $180 million IT budget, including $30 million in contracts and grants, and hired 1,300 full-time employees.

The following lists the contents of the chart in hierarchical order. At the top is the Vice President for IT and Chief Information Officer (VP for IT and CIO). Below the VP for IT and CIO are four personnel types : OVPIT Officers, OVPIT Division Leaders, Chief of Staff and Executive Assistant. Several subordinate units are organized under the OVPIT Officers and OVPIT Division Leaders respectively. The OVPIT Officers include seven officers, two of which have their unit's names listed below their titles. The OVPIT Division Leaders include eight division leaders of the UITS. For each division, the leader’s title is listed below the division’s name. With the exception of one division, the leader of each division respectively leads the division’s UITS staff at all IU campuses.
