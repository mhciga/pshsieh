IU Dining Job Aids (Union Street Market)

Overview

Indiana University Bloomington Dining plays an integral role in the day-to-day lives of members of the campus community. To aid in the efficient service of Dining customers, I have created two job aids for the Dining of the Union Street Center: a procedural infographic illustrating how to make a classic pizza and a set of image-based labels indicating the quantity of food per serving of sandwiches. These job aids were published in the IU Union Street Market to improve new employees’ work performance.

Challenge

Dining’s employees are responsible for providing fresh, healthy meals for customers. There are always new part-time students at Dining and in a short amount of time they have to learn how to prepare meals for customers, so a quick guide that shows new employees the right way to prepare food would be helpful.

Solution

The Dining employees must be able to quickly read and understand the various steps and components required for preparing meals. To help facilitate this process, I have created a procedural infographic as an alternative to a text-based document. This printable infographic can be printed out and posted in an easily visible location.

Process

  • Needs Analysis

    • After conducting an interview with the Dining's owner and manager and analyzing the Dining's official documents, I determined that the best way to meet their needs was to create a procedural infographic for making pizza and a set of image-based ingredients labels for making sandwiches. This decision was made after carefully considering the types of job aids they might need, as well as the employees' preferred delivery methods.

  • UIUX Design

    • I collaborated with the manager to identify ways to increase engagement with job aids among part-time student employees. What types of visuals and formats are most appealing to them? Additionally, what are the best locations to ensure they can access the job aids in a timely manner? Lastly, how can we best guide them to use the job aids?

  • Infographic Design

    • I created an infographic to illustrate the multiple steps and elements of preparing a pizza. Additionally, I created a series of image-based labels for ingredients to demonstrate the appropriate amounts of each ingredient for a sandwich. I used Adobe Capture to transfer sketches to digital devices and then completed all the work with Adobe Illustrator.

Results

The job aids were provided to the IU Union Street Market to serve as a reference for new employees.
