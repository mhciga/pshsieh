Procedural infographic - How to Make a Classic Pizza

The procedural infographic contains eight steps for making a classic pizza. The first row includes four steps and the second row includes four steps as well, from left to right, top to bottom are:

  1. Take a pizza crust.
  2. Spread 1 scoop of tomato sauce on the crust.
  3. Tear and spread 4 pieces of Mozzarella on the crust.
  4. Place 9 pieces of pepperoni on the crust.
  5. Toast the pizza in an oven.
    (One pizza: 1 minute 42 second; two pizza: 2 minutes 10 seconds)
  6. Place the toasted pizza in a box.
  7. Spread 1 sprinkle of black pepper, basil, and salt on the toasted pizza.
  8. Cut the pizza to 4 pieces

A classic pizza has been be finished through following these eight steps.
